Acoustic Impedance [5 marks]

(c) What happens when impedance mismatch is large? [2]
Mark Scheme▼
MS(a) Z = ρv [1]

(c) Large mismatch → strong reflection / most ultrasound reflected [2]

Coupling Gel [4 marks]
(a) Why is coupling gel essential in ultrasound imaging? [2]
(b) What would happen without gel? Explain using acoustic impedance. [2]
Mark Scheme▼
MS(a) Eliminates air gap / matches impedance / allows ultrasound into body [2]
(b) Tissue vs air: very large impedance mismatch → 99% reflected at skin surface [2]

A-scan [6 marks]
(a) How does A-scan determine depth of a boundary? [2]
(b) Pulse takes 0.15 ms to return. c=1540 m/s. Find depth. [3]
(c) Why pulsed rather than continuous? [1]
Mark Scheme▼
MS(a) Pulse sent & reflected; time delay measured; depth = cΔt/2 [2]
(b) Depth = 1540 × 0.15×10⊃−3;/2 = 0.115 m = 11.5 cm [3]
(c) Transducer cannot emit and detect simultaneously [1]

PET Process [6 marks]

(b) Energy and direction of emitted radiation. [2]
(c) Why must PET tracers have short half-lives? [1]
